Our Signature Events

Celebrations that define the BANF community experience

Flagship Event

Durga Puja - Our Crown Jewel

শারদীয় দুর্গোৎসব • ২০০৯ থেকে

Since 2009, BANF has been organizing one of the most elaborate Durga Puja celebrations in Northeast Florida. Our 4-day festival brings together the entire Bengali community for:

  • Traditional Puja rituals with professional priests
  • Beautiful idol crafted by Kumartuli artisans
  • Dhunuchi dance & Sindur Khela
  • Cultural programs & community bhog

Saraswati Puja - Celebrating Learning

সরস্বতী পূজা • হাতে খড়ি

The worship of Goddess Saraswati holds special significance for our community's children. Our celebration includes:

  • Traditional Puja ceremony
  • Hate Khori - initiation to learning for children
  • Students' cultural performances
  • Prasad & community gathering
