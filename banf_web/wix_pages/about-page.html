Our Mission & Values

Building bridges between generations and preserving our cultural heritage

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Bengali literature, art, music, and cultural heritage while fostering a sense of community among Bengalis in North Florida.

Our Vision

To be the leading cultural organization that bridges generations, preserves traditions, and creates a vibrant Bengali community experience in Jacksonville.

Our Values

Unity, respect for tradition, cultural preservation, community service, and passing our rich heritage to future generations with love and pride.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a thriving cultural community

2008
BANF Founded

Bengali Association of North Florida was established as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization in Jacksonville.

2009
First Durga Puja

Organized our inaugural Durga Puja celebration, bringing the Jacksonville Bengali community together.

2012
Community Growth

Membership grew to 100+ families. Expanded cultural programming with Saraswati Puja and Pohela Boishakh.

2015
Anandadhara Launched

Started our signature annual cultural extravaganza "Anandadhara" - Stream of Joy.

2019
10th Anniversary Durga Puja

Celebrated a decade of Durga Puja with record attendance and special cultural programs.

2020
Digital Adaptation

Pivoted to virtual events during the pandemic, keeping the community connected through online celebrations.

2022
Post-Pandemic Revival

Returned to in-person celebrations with renewed enthusiasm and community spirit.

2025
New Era Begins

Launching modernized digital platform, expanding community outreach, and planning for future growth.
